ABOUT ME

My name is Jackson Monk, and I am majoring in New Media and minoring in Graphic Design at Augsburg University in Minneapolis, Minnesota. As a visual learner, I see the value of storytelling through graphic images. I am excited to grow as a graphic designer and share eye-cathing, creative, and relatable branding messages. New Media strengthens my design messaging by giving me experience with generating articulate, meaningful expressions in digital environments.


I have taken the following courses:

    • Design Studio I that functioned as a design agency with a client

    • Design principles coursework, including typography, color theory, visual hierarchy, and layout using Adobe Cloud

    • Front-end developer coursework in HTML, CSS, and some JavaScript/jQuery

    • Graphic Systems that explored design concepts for interactive experiences

    • Animation that focused on practice, theory, and cultural function of animation in film, television, and new media tools.
