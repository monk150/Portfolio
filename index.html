ABOUT ME

My name is Jackson Monk, and I am majoring in New Media and minoring in Graphic Design at Augsburg University in Minneapolis, Minnesota. As a visual learner, I see the value of storytelling through graphic images. I am excited to grow as a graphic designer and share a brand’s message. New Media strengthens my design messaging by giving me experience with generating articulate, meaningful expressions in digital environments.


I have taken the following courses:

  • Web Fundamentals that focused on HTML and CSS

  • Design Studio I that functioned as a design agency with a client

  • Graphic Systems that explored design concepts for interactive experiences

  • Animation that focused on After Effects

  • Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign

    • My inspiration comes from pop culture, current events, and nature. I am excited to learn new skills and grow as a graphic designer.
